Our Company
We are a leading industrial distribution, services and systems integration company in Nigeria.
Global Presence
Our affiliation with top manufacturers across the globe makes us stand out as a reliable partner with access to wide range of industrial and engineering spare parts.
12+ years
We have grown to become an engineering service support company with technical capabilities to enable us support our customers.
With our global presence, we deliver original technical spare parts nationwide, sourced from Original Equipment Manufacturers.

We started out in 2009 as a mainly engineering procurement company and over the years we have grown to become an engineering service support company with technical capabilities to enable us support our customers in such areas as; selection, sizing, installation, commissioning, modification, retrofitting, troubleshooting, repairs, calibration and instrument verification.
